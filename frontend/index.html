How It Works

01

Feature Extraction

VGG19 convolutional layers extract spatial content features from the content image and texture statistics from the style reference.

02

Gram Matrix Loss

Style is encoded as Gram matrices of feature maps — capturing texture correlations independent of spatial layout.

03

L-BFGS Optimization

The output image is optimized directly via L-BFGS to minimize a weighted sum of content and style losses simultaneously.
